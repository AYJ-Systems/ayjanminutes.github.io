How it works

01

Records your meeting

Captures system audio and microphone simultaneously. Start recording with one click — no setup, no integrations.

02

Transcribes locally

Transcription runs locally on your own machine. Your audio never touches a server — fast, private, and processed entirely on your PC.

03

Summarises with AI

Sends only the transcript to Claude or ChatGPT. Generates structured meeting minutes with action items and decisions.

04

Detects attendees and meeting context. Saves your notes with a meaningful filename — no manual renaming needed.

Your audio
stays yours

Most AI meeting tools send your audio to their servers for transcription. Ayjan Minutes does not. Transcription runs entirely on your own computer — your audio never leaves your machine.

Built for people who care about the privacy of their conversations. Whether it's a confidential client call, a sensitive team discussion, or a personal session — what's said in the room stays on your device.

The only data that ever leaves your machine is the text transcript — and only when you choose to generate a summary. You pick the AI provider. You control your data.
